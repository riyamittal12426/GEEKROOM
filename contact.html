Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining GeekRoom

How can I join GeekRoom Adgips?

Simply fill out the contact form above or join our Discord server. We welcome all skill levels and backgrounds! Our community is open to anyone passionate about technology and learning.

Is there a membership fee?

No, GeekRoom Adgips is completely free to join. We believe in making technology education accessible to everyone and removing financial barriers to learning.

What events do you organize?

We organize weekly workshops, monthly hackathons, tech talks, project showcases, networking events, coding competitions, and collaborative learning sessions across various technology domains.

Can beginners join?

Absolutely! We have programs designed for all skill levels, from complete beginners to advanced developers. Our mentors provide personalized guidance to help you grow at your own pace.

Do you offer mentorship?

Yes, we have experienced mentors who provide guidance on projects, career development, technical skills, and personal growth in the technology industry.

How often are events held?

We have regular weekly activities, with special events monthly. Check our Discord server and events page for the latest schedule and upcoming opportunities.
